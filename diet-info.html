Diet for scottish fold


Protein Cats are obligate carnivores, meaning that they need animal-based protein to meet their nutritional needs.
Fat Fat provides energy and helps to keep your cat's skin and coat healthy. Look for cat food that includes healthy fats, sych as omega-3 and omega-6 fatty acids.
Carbs While cats don't require carbohydrates, some cat foods do contain them. So be sure to look for food contains high-quality, easily digestible carbs such as brown rice or sweet potato.
Vitamins and Minerals Search for cat food that contains a balanced blend of vitamins and minerals to make sure your Scottish Fold gets all their needed nutrients.
